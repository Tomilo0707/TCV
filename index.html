Dr. Moringe L. Parkipuny

The First Maasai to represent Ngorongoro
in the National Assembly.

He was among the first Son of Maa society to get formal education.
He was also the first Maasai to be elected to the National parliament.
Though he was smart, his fate was not good as he later suffered mental illness.
He passed away in 1990s.
